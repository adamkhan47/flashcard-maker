Flashcard Maker


Make flashcards, use a Quizlet flashcard. Built for Flashcard Loader



To use Quizlet: Make sure you own a Quizlet (copy it or make one) and then click the three dots and click Export.
After that, paste in the text you got there into the box and click Import from Quizlet.
Or, put in your term first, seperate it with "|", then put in the definition.
