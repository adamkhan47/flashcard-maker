Flashcard Maker


Make flashcards, or use a existing Quizlet flashcard. Built for Flashcard Reader



To use Quizlet: Make sure you own a Quizlet (copy it or make one) and then click the three dots on the top right of the flashcard page and click Export.
After that, paste in the text you got there into the box and click Import from Quizlet.


Or, in the box, put in your term first, separate it with "|" with no spaces, then put in the definition.
